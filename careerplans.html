Summary

There are a number of similarities and differences between the career plans of the members of Kaalab. Generally speaking each member still intends to move into the same area they expressed an interest in through assignments 1 and 2, with most members suggesting a shift within the same broad categories. This observation should be qualified by saying that we are all aware that we will be exposed to many areas within the IT industry in the coming years, and we remain open to pursuing different avenues should we feel it appropriate.

The members of Kaalab fall into two broad categories. Angelo, Adrian and Aidan have expressed an interest in hardware and infrastructure based roles, whilst Kieran, Bryan and Luca are more interested in software development and programming.
