UX Developer

Quest                                                                                                                                                                                                      Posted 10/05/2022

Virtual Position – WFH

Information & Communication

$100,000 – Temporary, 12 Month Max Term Contract.

 

Mythos is a start-up partnership that is presently self-funded online business that provides services to authors, players and business owners involved in Dungeons and Dragons.

Due to the recent success of the business; Mythos has recently obtained significant funding from charitable investors looking to see Mythos become more widespread and expand on the number of services provided.

To assist with this the Partners are looking for a UX Developer to provide their skills to assist the business.

 

The mandatory skills required

  • Strong design skills: Create, solve, and iterate
  • Skilled with digital prototyping tools.
  • Knows about lean start-up, lean UX design: We don’t make specs, we explore, and evolve with the team.
  • Has worked with user empathy tools like personas, jobs to be done, etc
  • Has worked with business design tools like business model canvas, value proposition design, lean canvas
  • Has worked with design collaboration tools like journey maps, impact maps, and loves a good design sprint
  • Experience creating and facilitating User Research and/or User Testing sessions
  • Previous UX experience in a digital environment
  • Experience working in agile environments
  • Bonus points for HTML, CSS, and Java Script knowledge
  • Experience in a SaaS / Software product environment preferred
  • Meticulous visuals and implementation
  • The ability to create user flows, wireframes, sketches, and prototypes through to high-resolution mock-ups and design assets
  • Proficiency using design software’s (e.g., sketch, InVision and Adobe Creative Cloud apps)
  • A thorough understanding of the emerging technologies within your area of expertise
  • Experience in product, interaction and visual design along with experience in responsive/adaptive design (preferred)
  • Proven ability to work in a fast-paced development environment
  • Demonstrated problem solving and diagnostic skills
  • The ability to manage priorities and schedule tasks with minimal direction
  • Strong organisational skills with high attention to detail
  • Experience in presenting design ideas and prototypes to internal and external stakeholders

 

More about you

  • You have the ability to work in a team and effectively communicate
  • You are self-motivated and able to work in solitary conditions for long periods
  • You have solid written and oral communicating skills
  • You are research focused - maintain, lead, and execute research/workshops
  • You have a keen ability to identify and articulate core needs and design priorities
  • You take ownership of work - autonomous as well as collaborative (working closely with product, business analysts and other designers)
  • If you have an Interest or knowledge of Dungeons and Dragons and/or novel writing that would be seen as favourable but not necessary

 

If you are interested and feel you have the necessary skills, please apply with button below and provide your Resume and Cover Letter expressing your interest in the role. We look forward to your application and look forward with potentially working with you towards the growth and future of this business.
