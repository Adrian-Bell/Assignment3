Full Stack Developer

Quest                                                                                                                                                                                       Posted 10/05/2022

Virtual Position – WFH

Information & Communication

$100,000 – Temporary, 12 Month Max Term Contract.

 

Mythos is a start-up partnership that is presently self-funded online business that provides services to authors, players and business owners involved in Dungeons and Dragons.

Due to the recent success of the business; Mythos has recently obtained significant funding from charitable investors looking to see Mythos become more widespread and expand on the number of services provided.

To assist with this the Partners are looking for a Full Stack Developer to provide their skills to assist the business.

 

The mandatory skills required

  • Minimum 3-5 years commercial experience
  • Experienced in using React.js, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, HTML5, PHP, Laravel, Python and NodeJS
  • Experienced in writing high-performance, reusable code for UI components including testing
  • Experienced in relational databases using languages such as MySQL, MS SQL, and MongoDB
  • Proficient in AWS, Linux
  • Effective use of git, and issue tracking tools
  • Demonstrated experience of thriving in a fast-paced entrepreneurial environment
  • Use of continuous integration and testing systems
  • Front-end application design & implementation – Desktop / Mobile / Web
  • Understanding of design principles and design patterns
  • Proven track record of delivering and executing projects within tight deadlines

 

More about you

  • You are open minded and curious.
  • You are a strong and creative communicator
  • You are friendly, approachable, adept at building authentic relationships
  • You are supportive and appreciative of different perspectives – you understand that our differences make us stronger together.
  • You are a skilled developer with a passion for front-end development. You don’t hate HTML or CSS
  • You are up to date with the latest front-end technologies
  • You have a solid understanding and extensive experience with JavaScript
  • You have experience working in the modern JavaScript ecosystem: ES6, Modules, Node.js, Webpack, SCSS
  • You have a good understanding of scalable CSS architecture
  • You have experience with software development tools such as Git, Docker, CI/CD, and AWS
  • You are not a designer, but you have a keen eye for beautiful UI and intuitive UX
  • If you have an Interest or knowledge of Dungeons and Dragons and/or novel writing that would be seen as favourable but not necessary

 

If you are interested and feel you have the necessary skills, please apply with button below and provide your Resume and Cover Letter expressing your interest in the role. We look forward to your application and look forward with potentially working with you towards the growth and future of this business.
